MISSION

Infina was established to advance patients’ health and quality of life through the supply and transmission of medical knowledge to the medical community, which will ultimately reduce the care gaps between regions and improve the quality of service provided to patients.


To help medical professionals advance their knowledge, skills, attitudes, and behaviors, and thus the clinical outcomes for their patients, Infina strives to provide newly discovered clinical and research information in appropriate educational formats.


Infina’s strategy incorporates knowledge management, which has been described as ‘getting the right knowledge to the right people in the right place at the right time.’ Knowledge must reach the participants who need it, when they need it, given the rapid rate at which patients’ expectations and the industry landscape changes.


Right knowledge

We discover and present the latest and recent knowledge and its applications to various levels of health care and disease prevention.

Right format

Depending on the subject or topics of lecture, we design and develop learning contents to maximize learning experience and level of concentration.

Right timing

We aim to provide participants with content that meets their needs and circumstances.

What We Do




Sourcing and reproduction of CME contents

We continuously source new knowledge through various types of partnerships with the world’s leading medical institutions and disseminate the collected contents via our online CME platform. Before the foreign contents are populated, we perform audiovisual translation and subtitling processes.

Operation of online CME platform

We build and deliver a world-class continuing medical education program through an online platform. Participants may obtain credits and certificates upon completion of courses to fulfill their licensure requirements.

Research and Publication

We publish educational contents for continuing medical education programs upon conducting a thorough analysis on the needs of targeted audience and medicine landscape.

Planning of Symposia and medical conferences

We organize international medical symposia and conferences throughout the year. The programs may be approved for CME credits and are a great opportunity for practitioners to connect globally and become part of the community, as well for exposure to new discoveries in medicine.

BUSINESS




Our Business in China

As part of a broader push to advance the nation’s health services, China’s Department of Capacity Building and Continuing Education Center (under the National Health and Family Planning Commission of the People’s Republic of China or NHFPC) is establishing an online-centered Continuing Medical Education system and appointing IEGOT Limited as the official operator of the platform. In November 2016, Infina signed an exclusive agreement with IEGOT to provide educational contents to be studied through this platform for the next 20 years.

This CME platform, ready to go live by the fall of 2018, will be a major source of professional education for 13 million practitioners across all fields of medicine for further development of their professional knowledge and skills.
